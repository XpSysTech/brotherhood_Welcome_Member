Constitution & Rules

Plain-language rules that keep the Brotherhood aligned and protected. These are the baseline standards while the full charter is finalized.

Alignment
Core principles +
We operate on trust, discipline, consistency, respect, and long-term thinking. Members protect the name of the Brotherhood through conduct and reliability.
Member responsibilities +
Members participate actively, communicate clearly, honor commitments, support other members, and uphold confidentiality where required.
Governance rules +
Decisions follow defined processes. Major decisions require discussion and documented agreement. Leadership exists for coordination and accountability — not unchecked control.
Capital handling rules +
Capital figures shown here are informational only and updated manually. Any use of pooled funds must be documented and approved according to the Brotherhood’s agreed process. Official records remain offline.
Privacy & security +
No authentication, payments, tracking, or sensitive personal data on this page. This reduces risk while the Brotherhood is still forming.
Note: This page is not a legal document. Final policies will be consolidated into a formal charter and constitution document.
